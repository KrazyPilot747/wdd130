Client Report

As the client, I am pleased with my project. It looks great and is functional. The devs communicated well with me and did a great job. I communicated with the devs in class. I chose the images for my site using google and the text was AI. I chose to do a website on Boeing because aviation has always been an intrest of mine. Next time I would make a more detailed and organized site plan.

Lead Dev Report

As a lead dev, I communicated with my junior devs in class though there was little to communicate. The client gave me almost nothing to worl with and wasnt in class very often. I styled up the main page, but that was about all that could be done. The site is published on github. Overall github was pretty intuitive and didnt give me too many issues. I would say it was a positive experience.

Junior Dev

I was lucky as a junior dev, both of my senior devs were very responsive and we communicated in class. Both projects I worked on had very clear siteplans. I didnt completly finish on my two sites, but they both are 85% of the way there.

General Reflections

What this project taugh me is that communication is tough. Its hard to work when the client wont tell you what they want. It was cool try to make someone elses vision work on my other projects though. Its interesting to see the ideas other people come up with. I dont know if I feel confident working on a real world project, but I feel more confident as a client. If I need a website designed, I will know what to expect of my client. Next time I work on a project, I will make sure I get a comprehesive site plan before agreeeing to the project.
